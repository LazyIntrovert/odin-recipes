Hta-ma-ne

Viscous glutinous snack

Hta-ma-ne goes well with the cold weather of the harvest season.The snacj=k is called hta-ma-ne because a large amount of sesame seeds is requried to make it.

Ingredients

  1. glutinous rice
  2. roasted seasame seeds
  3. roasted peanuts
  4. coconut slices
  5. a generous amout of cooking oil
  1. The glutinous rice will require soaking in water for about 2 hours.

  2. The shredded coconut will also need to be fried till crispy, care is required to not over cook and cause it to burn.

  3. Also fry up the peanuts and some sliced ginger. Sieve the oil and leave to drain.

  4. When you are ready heat up a wok or frying pan with oil. You will require a large wok as you will be putting the rice in to here as well.

  5. once the oil is sizzling add in the ginger and give it a quick stir.

  6. Now add in the garlic and stir-fry until they are fragrant and golden brown.

  7. Add in the glutinous rice. Add in water and stir gently. Sprinkle in salt.

  8. Cover the pan and let the rice cook for about 30 mins. Use a medium heat. Stir will from the from the bottom of the pan to make sure its not sticking. Check by taste to make sure the rice has softened.

  9. Once the rice is cooked you may add the peanuts, sesame seeds, shredded coconut. Also extra crispy ginger and garlic can be added to suit your taste.

  10. Use two large spatulas to fold and knead the rice and ingredients. This may take 15- 20 minutes.

  11. Once everything is evenly mixed in and rice is similar to a ball of dough its ready.
